About me

Hello, I am Diego and I am a web developer. I live and work in Poland. I left the paradise beaches and nice weather of Canary Islands in order to persuit my career as a developer somewhere where I'm not surrounded by a giant ocean...

Since the time Covid striked, I spend most of my time learning new stuff, experimenting with HTML, CSS and JS

What I enjoy the most about coding is the rewarding feeling you achieve when you have successfully done something, after maybe struggling for a while, even going deeply into the rabbit hole, but eventually coming out, with a lesson learned and the joy of having created something by yourself, something that brings value to you or your customer.
